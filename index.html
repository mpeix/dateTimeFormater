Date Time formatter

Type a date time format an see how to write it in multiple languages.

There is plenty of options to format the date and time. View some of them here.

This page uses Momment Js => to provide the date time format in multiple culture formats.

Usefull date time formats

Year

  • YYYY four digit year
  • YY two digit year
  • Y year

Month

  • M, MM month number
  • MMM month shortened form
  • MMMM month name

Day

  • D, DD day of month
  • Do day with ordinal
  • ddd, dddd day name (Mon ... Monday)
  • e day of week
  • DDD, DDDD day of year

Others

  • w, ww week of year
  • X Unix timestamp
  • DDD, DDDD day of year
